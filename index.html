Hello, welcome to the United States Anomalous Containment Agency.

If you have not figured this out already, this is a wiki similar to the SCP Foundation wiki.

This wiki is to host articles of the anomalous. We do not have any hatred towards the SCP Foundation or anything, we just wanted a place to host our works. Please take a look at the previously researched Anomalous objects on the left.

If you wish to submit a report for a new Anomalous object, Click here.

Enjoy!
